about

Graphic Designer / Artist / Web-Developer

I’m an Artist with a degree/Diploma in Graphic Design and Web Development. My expertise lies in Brand/Identity Design, Production Art, and Dynamic Website Design. Well-versed (Experienced) in Project Management and Marketing Strategies. I foremost like to pay attention to difficulties with usability and accessibility issues, as well as typography, color theory, layout, and composition. Frameworks, HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Photoshop, Indesign, and Illustrator are all things I’m familiar with. I am passionate about teaching. Born in Teheran/Iran I now live in Berlin/Germany and speak English and German in addition to Farsi.
