about

Graphic Designer / Artist / Web-Developer

Nina Ansari is a Graphic Designer and Web-Developer with a skill-set in a production art, brand/idententy design and dynamic website design. Well versed in marketing campaings and project managment. Attentive to typography, color theory, layout/composition, as well as accessibility and usability issues. Familiar with frameworks, HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator. She has a passion for teaching. She speaks english, german and farsi and lives in Berlin.

Trained in photography, painting and film at the HfG Academy of Art and Design in Offenbach am Main, Nina Ansari developed a varied practice including book and installation to further investigate the different kinds of artistic expression and media. Through sophisticated systems, we discover her attraction to the idea of the individual in the modern society and their unique characteristics, in particular, the concept of identity as an area of contact between the self and the Other. Born in 1981 Teheran/ Iran. Lives in Berlin.
