about

Graphic Designer / Artist / Web-Developer

Nina Ansari is a Graphic Designer/Web-Developer with a skill-set in a production art, brand/idententy design and dynamic website design. Well versed in marketing campaings and project managment. Attentive to typography, color theory, layout/composition, as well as accessibility and usability issues. Familiar with frameworks, HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator. She has a passion for teaching. She speaks english, german and farsi and lives in Berlin.
