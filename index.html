about

Graphic Designer / Artist / Web-Developer

Nina Ansari is a Graphic Designer and Web-Developer with skill-set in a production art, brand/identnty design and dynamic website design. Well versed in marketing campaings and project managment. Attentive to typography, color theory, layout/composition, as well as accessibility and usability issues. Familiar with frameworks, HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator. She has a passion for teaching. She speaks english, german and farsi and lives in Berlin. Trained in photography, painting and film at the HfG Academy of Art and Design in Offenbach am Main, Nina Ansari developed a varied practice including book and installation to further investigate the different kinds of artistic expression and media. Through sophisticated systems, we discover her attraction to the idea of the individual in the modern society and their unique characteristics, in particular, the concept of identity as an area of contact between the self and the Other. Born in 1981 Teheran/ Iran. Lives in Berlin. Nina Ansari´s Œuvre ist vielschichtig und umfasst die Medien Fotografie, Film, Installation und Malerei. Die 1981 in Teheran geborene Künstlerin thematisiert in ihren Arbeiten immer wieder autobiographische Erinnerungen. Durch ausgeklügelte Systeme, entdecken wir ihre Anziehungskraft auf die Idee des Individuums in der modernen Gesellschaft und ihren einzigartigen Eigenschaften, insbesondere der Begriff der Identität als einen Raum der Kontakt zwischen dem Selbst und dem Anderen. Sie lebt in Berlin.
